# Warranty & Guarantee Terms

Labor and parts warranties for residential and commercial customers.
## Labor Warranty
All labor: 90-day warranty from date of service, no charge to return.
Major installs (water heater, furnace, AC unit, panel upgrade): 1-year labor warranty.
## Parts Warranty
Meridian-supplied parts: passed-through manufacturer warranty (typically 1–5 years).
Customer-supplied parts: no Meridian warranty on parts; labor warranty still applies.
## Maintenance Plan Members
Plan Labor Warranty Extension T1 Repairs
Silver Standard 90 days Not covered
Gold Extended to 1 year on all repairs Not covered
Platinum Extended to 1 year on all repairs Covered at no charge
## Exclusions
Damage from misuse, flooding, fire, freeze, or acts of God.
Pest-related damage.
Issues arising from customer alterations made after service.
## Claim Process
Customer calls or emails to initiate a warranty claim. Agent creates a Warranty Return job in the Booking system with
job_type = 'warranty_return'. No diagnostic fee is charged for warranty visits.
