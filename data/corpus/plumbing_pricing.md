# Plumbing Pricing Bands

Common plumbing services and emergency dispatch pricing.
## Service Call Fee
$75 flat — applied toward any repair over $200 booked on the same visit.
## Common Services
Service Price Range Notes
Drain clearing (standard) $120 – $200 Hydro-jet add $95
Faucet / fixture replacement $150 – $350 Parts extra if customer-supplied excluded
Toilet repair / replace $175 – $450 Parts included for standard models
Water heater replacement (40 gal) $950 – $1,400 Tankless: quote required
Slab/pipe leak detection $300 – $500 Camera inspection extra $150
Main shutoff / PRV replacement $400 – $750 Permit may be required
## Emergency Plumbing (Active Water Leak)
Flat $99 emergency dispatch fee. After-hours surcharges same as HVAC policy.
