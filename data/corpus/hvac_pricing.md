# HVAC Pricing Bands

Ballpark pricing for diagnostics, repairs, and maintenance plans. All prices in USD.
## Diagnostic Fee
$89 flat — waived when repair is booked on the same day.
## Repair Tiers
Tier Description Price Range
T1 Minor (filter swap, thermostat calibration, capacitor) $95 – $250
T2 Moderate (fan motor, contactor, refrigerant top-up £2 lb) $250 – $650
T3 Major (compressor, coil, refrigerant >2 lb, ductwork section) $650 – $2,400
T4 Full system replacement — refer to Sales for quoted scope Quote required
## Maintenance Plans
Plan Annual Fee Includes
Silver $149 / yr 1× tune-up, 10% repair discount
Gold $249 / yr 2× tune-ups, 15% repair discount, priority scheduling
Platinum $399 / yr Unlimited tune-ups, 20% discount, same-day priority, free T1 repairs
## After-Hours Surcharge
+$75 for calls before 7 am or after 6 pm Monday–Saturday.
+$125 on Sundays and federal holidays.
