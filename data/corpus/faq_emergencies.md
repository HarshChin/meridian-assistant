# FAQ — Emergencies & After-Hours

What qualifies as an emergency, how to request urgent service, and SLA targets.
## What Counts as an Emergency?
Active water leak or flooding
No heat when outdoor temperature is forecast below 40°F
No cooling when indoor temperature exceeds 95°F
Electrical hazard: burning smell, sparking, or partial power loss
Sewage backup
How to Request Emergency Service
Call 1-800-555-0190 (24/7). Do NOT use the web booking portal for emergencies — response is not guaranteed
through that channel.
Emergency Response Time SLA
Member Tier Target ETA
Platinum 2 hours or less
Gold 3 hours or less
Silver / Non-member 4 hours or less (best effort)
After-Hours Surcharges
See the HVAC and Plumbing pricing documents for full surcharge details. Emergency dispatch fees ($99 plumbing,
$89 HVAC) are not waived for after-hours calls.
