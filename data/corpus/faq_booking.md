# FAQ — Booking & Scheduling

Answers to the most common questions about appointments, windows, and reminders.
## How far in advance can I book?
Up to 60 days. Same-day slots are available before 11 am for Gold and Platinum members, or before 9 am for
standard customers.
## Can I request a specific technician?
Yes — use the preferred_tech field when booking. Availability is not guaranteed; the system will notify the customer if
the preferred tech is unavailable.
## How long does a typical visit take?
Service Type Typical Duration
HVAC tune-up 60 – 90 min
HVAC diagnostic 45 – 60 min
Plumbing service call 60 – 120 min
Electrical inspection 60 – 90 min
Full system install 4 – 8 hours
## Will I get a reminder?
Yes — automated SMS and email reminders are sent 24 hours before the appointment and 30 minutes before the
technician departs.
## What is the appointment window?
All appointments use 2-hour windows (e.g., 10 am – 12 pm). The technician sends an ETA when en route.
