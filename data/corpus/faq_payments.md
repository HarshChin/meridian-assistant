# FAQ — Payments & Invoicing

Payment methods, estimates, financing, and invoice dispute process.
## When do I pay?
Payment is due at completion of service. The technician processes payment on-site via card, tap-to-pay, or check.
Net-30 invoicing is available for commercial accounts only.
## What payment methods do you accept?
Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover · Apple Pay / Google Pay · Personal check (residential) or business check
(commercial) · Zelle (residential customers only, up to $500) · Cash (discouraged — technician may not carry
change).
## Can I get an estimate before the visit?
Phone estimates are ballpark only, based on pricing bands. Written estimates are provided on-site after the
diagnostic. Customer must approve before work begins.
## Is financing available?
Yes — for jobs over $1,000 via GreenSky. 0% for 12 months with approved credit. Apply online or on-site with the
technician.
## How do I dispute an invoice?
Email billing@meridian-home.com within 30 days of the service date. The billing team responds within 2 business
days.
