# Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

Fee schedule for customer cancellations, no-shows, and Meridian-initiated changes.
## Standard Cancellation Fees
Notice Given Fee
More than 24 hours before appointment No fee
2 to 24 hours before appointment $35 late-cancel fee
Less than 2 hours or no-show $75 no-show fee (waived once per 12-month period per customer)
## Rescheduling
More than 24 hours notice: free, unlimited reschedules.
Same-day reschedule: treated as a late-cancel if the new slot is not the same day.
## Meridian-Initiated Cancellations
If Meridian cancels with less than 4 hours notice, customer receives a $25 credit toward their next booking.
Weather-related cancellations: no fee to customer; re-book at no charge.
## Fee Disputes
Customers who dispute a cancellation fee must be escalated to the Branch Manager. Agents must NOT waive fees
unilaterally, except for the once-per-12-months first-time no-show waiver.
