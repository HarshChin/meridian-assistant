# Electrical Pricing Bands

Common electrical services. All prices in USD, labor only unless noted.
## Service Call Fee
$85 flat — waived when repair total exceeds $300.
## Common Services
Service Price Range
Outlet / switch replacement $95 – $175
GFCI / AFCI installation (per outlet) $120 – $200
Ceiling fan install (fixture supplied by customer) $150 – $250
Panel inspection + report $175 – $300
Circuit breaker replacement $200 – $400
Sub-panel installation $800 – $1,800
Full panel upgrade (100A to 200A) $1,800 – $3,500
EV charger — Level 2, NEMA 14-50 $450 – $900
